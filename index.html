About Us

Welcome to NyayaGPT, an AI-powered legal assistant designed to simplify access to Indian legal knowledge. Our project is built with the vision of making legal information more accessible, understandable, and free for everyone. Whether you are a student, researcher, lawyer, or a citizen seeking clarity on legal matters, NyayaGPT is here to provide quick and reliable answers based on Indian law.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex legal texts and public understanding by utilizing advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and machine learning techniques. By keeping the project open and free to use, we hope to empower individuals with the legal knowledge they need without the barriers of expensive consultations or complicated legal jargon. .
